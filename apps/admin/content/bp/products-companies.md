### Organization Profiles

1. **Content:**

   - **Included Information:**
     - Founding date
     - Organization name
     - Short description
     - Vision
     - Founding team
     - Funding
     - Employee count
     - Job opportunities
     - Events/public engagement
     - Products/services
     - Social media links
     - Industry category
   - **Selection Criteria:**
     - Must be a registered organization with a website.
     - Must be in the space/astronomy domain.
     - No defense contractors or political organizations.
     - Website review for inclusion.

2. **Features:**
   - **Interactive Features:**
     - News updates from organization blogs/newsrooms.
     - Social media content scraping and display (paid feature, free for space agencies).
     - Job listings from organization websites/LinkedIn.
     - Event and product scraping if available.

### Organization Profiles

Detailed profiles of space and astronomy organizations are available on AstronEra, featuring information
such as founding date, organization name, vision, team, funding, employee count, job opportunities, and
social media links. Only registered organizations in the space/astronomy domain are included, ensuring a
focus on relevant and credible organizations. Interactive features keep users updated on news,
events, and job listings from these organizations.
